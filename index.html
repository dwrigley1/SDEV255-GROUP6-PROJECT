Courses Offered

ENGL 111 - English Composition - 3 Credit Hours

Develops skills in writing, reading, critical thinking, and research with emphasis on academic writing forms.

ENGL 202 - Creative Writing - 3 Credit Hours

Prerequisites - ENGL 111

Introduces opportunities for self-expression through fiction, poetry, drama, and creative nonfiction.

MATH 221 - Calculus for Technology I - 3 Credit Hours

Covers limits, continuity, derivatives, integrals, and their applications in technology.

MATH 222 - Calculus for Technology II - 3 Credit Hours

Prerequisites - MATH 221

Covers transcendental functions, integration techniques, differential equations, and infinite series.

BIOL 121 - General Biology I - 3 Credit Hours

Introduces cell structure, photosynthesis, genetics, and biotechnology in prokaryotes and eukaryotes.

BIOL 122 - General Biology II - 3 Credit Hours

Prerequisites - BIOL 121

Explores evolution, plant and animal function, anatomy, physiology, behavior, and ecology.
